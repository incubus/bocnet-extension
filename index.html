    Chrome and Safari extension for limited access to the personal banking system of Bank of China (http://www.boc.cn)

    Project maintained by incubus Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    BOCNET access

    This extension provides you a limited access to the personal banking system of Bank of China (中国银行) from Chrome and Safari browsers.

    It is based on the work and ideas from np-activex project and Tianji Wu

    Icon design by Vitaly Usov

    Any help is welcome!

    Information

    Bank of China website: http://www.boc.cn/en

    BOCNET personal banking system: http://www.boc.cn/ebanking/bocnet_login/
